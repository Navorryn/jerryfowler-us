Who we are

People behind
the domain

Real humans, real email addresses. Jerry runs a handful of companies; Beth keeps everything from going sideways. Together they've built a life that mixes security operations with stoic philosophy, property management with national park trails, and strong coffee with stronger opinions about books — all shared with two very opinionated dogs.

J

Jerry Fowler

Founder · CISO · Operator

Jerry runs three companies — a cybersecurity services firm, a property and asset management operation, and a small press — while also doing web work for select clients. He serves as a fractional CISO for organizations that need serious security leadership without the overhead. When he's not in operator mode, he's reading Marcus Aurelius, hiking a trail somewhere, or mid-project on something made of either code or hardware.
